Frequently Asked Questions

How does a yard sign rental work?

We deliver and set up your customized yard sign display at your chosen address. Signs stay up for the rental period, then we return to pick them up. Payment secures your date.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 72 hours in advance. Orders within 72 hours incur a $35 rush fee. Popular dates (graduations, holidays) book up fast — earlier is always better!

What occasions do you cover?

Birthdays, graduations, anniversaries, baby showers, retirements, military homecomings, cancer survivorship, quinceañeras, and more. If it's worth celebrating, we're there!

What's your cancellation policy?

Full refund (minus $5) for cancellations 96+ hours out. 75% refund at 72 hrs, 50% at 48 hrs, 25% at 24 hrs. Bad weather may require a reschedule. See full terms →
